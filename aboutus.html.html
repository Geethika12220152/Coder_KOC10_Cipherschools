Alien University is a private university located in Chaheru, Phagwara, Punjab, India. The university was established in 2005 by Alien International Trust, under The Alien University Act, 2005 (Punjab Act 25 of 2005) and started operation in 2006.

CAMPUS

The university campus is spread over 600 acres in Chaheru Village on the Jalandhar-Delhi GT Road.

GOVERNANCE

The university is governed by chancellor Nivedika Namburi. The administration and operations are governed by Pro Chancellor of the institution Shahana.

AFFILIATIONS

ALIEN is affiliated with the University Grants Commission, the National Council for Teacher Education[9] and the Council of Architecture and approved by the Pharmacy Council of India. It is also a member of the Association of Indian Universities (AIU). The law programs are recognized by the Bar Council of India ALIEN's School of Agriculture is accredited by the Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR). Internationally, ALIEN is accredited by the Accreditation Council for Business Schools and Programs (ACBSP) and a member of the Association of Commonwealth Universities (ACU). The distance education programmes are approved by the Distance Education Bureau (DEB).
